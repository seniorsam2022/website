Objective

Why "Supérate Forever"? It assigned this name to show that Supérate will be forever; it will always be there to help us. It was created on November 1, 2022, to help Supérate students with their questions about the subjects taught in the classes, such as English, Computer Science, and Values, also helping them to better prepare for the Microsoft and TOEIC certificates. We hope this helpful tool will prosper forever for you, dear friends.

Message from the AM Seniors to you:

During these three years that we have been together, it has been one of the best experiences; we went through different situations, both good and evil, because to graduate, you have to be patient, intellectual, and persevering. It was challenging for all of us to graduate because of the amount of homework we were assigned both at school and in the course; although we knew it was going to happen, we were determined to face it because we knew it would be worth it. Sometimes we indeed felt incompetent, discouraged, tired, that we could not go on, and all kinds of negative feelings and thoughts, but if we put our hand down, if we let that consume us, all the effort we had made would not have been worth it, however, with the help of our Supérate teachers and our Supérate classmates we could make it, we encouraged each other, we helped each other, we promised to graduate together. So it was; we made it, and all our effort to get here was thanks to us. We leave this gift to you future Supérate graduates. We know this will help you to go the extra mile and achieve your goals.

Operation

This web page contains information about the courses taught in Supérate such as English, computer science and values, each class has its specific topics, for example, in the area of English we find information to improve vocabulary and grammar, also to prepare for the Toeic certification. In the computer area, we can find information about programming and excel, ways to create dynamic slides in PowerPoint or make formal letters in Word, and better prepare for Microsoft certifications. On the other hand, Values contain information to help them mentally and physically, more on a personal topic, how to be better students, prepare them for life, etc. In the blog area, you will find all the information on all the courses, with their respective tags. We also see a search bar that will allow you to search for information quickly by entering a word or phrase. Finally, at the bottom of the web page, you will find Supérate's social networks for more information. That's all for now.
